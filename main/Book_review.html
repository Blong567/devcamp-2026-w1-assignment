BOOK REVIEW

Project Hail Mary: an adventure to save the Earth

Project Hail Mary, a sci-fi book from Andy Weir, is a story of a middle-school and also a scientist called Rylan Grace on his journey to save the Earth from tiny, basic space creatures. Those creatures absord the energy from the sun to breeding. As a consequence, the Earth is dying. On the journey to find the cure for the Earth, Grace is not alone. He met another alien has human's intelligenc. Two different species from difference star systems accompany with each other to save their planet!

Beside the intriguing story above, one this that make this book special is its "scientific tone". The story is a masterclass in scientific problem-solving, as Grace uses physics, biology, and chemistry to navigate his mission. The narrative excels due to its "competence porn" style, where every obstacle is met with rigorous logic and experimentation.

Rate: 8/10 (In my oponion!!)
